OUTLINE
The primary goal is to produce a refined, concise, and engaging text that effectively conveys the original message.
Essential information, key ideas, and the original intent must be preserved.
Redundant sentences, unnecessary information, and repetitive concepts must be eliminated.
The language must be simplified using active voice, positive phrasing, and breaking down complex sentences.
Connections between ideas and paragraphs must be strengthened for a fluent logic.
Excessively long sentences must be rephrased to reduce their length while maintaining the meaning.
Readability must be optimized through correct grammar, concise syntax, and avoiding unclear sentences.
Superfluous elements like unnecessary words, filler language, or tangential contents must be omitted.
The desired result is a clear, engaging, and highly concise text that balances brevity and readability while keeping all essential information.
